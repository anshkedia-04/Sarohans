Craft & Fabric

Shop by Weave & Art

01
Bandhani
Ancient tie-dye craft from Rajasthan. Each piece is a unique work of art with intricate patterns formed by thousands of tiny bindings.
02
Pure Silk
Lustrous Kanjivaram and Banarasi silk woven with gold zari threads for an unmatched regal elegance.
03
Georgette
Light and flowy, perfect for evening occasions. The graceful drape of georgette flatters every silhouette.
04
Gota Patti
The royal embroidery of Rajasthan. Golden ribbons carefully applied to create stunning geometric patterns.

Handloom
Sustainably woven on traditional looms by master weavers, preserving India's irreplaceable textile heritage.
