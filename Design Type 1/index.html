Care Guide

Fabric Care Instructions

🌊
Silk & Zardozi
Dry clean only. Do not wring. Store in muslin cloth away from direct sunlight. Use camphor balls to preserve embroidery.
👐
Cotton & Block Print
Hand wash in cold water. Use mild detergent. Wash dark colours separately for first 2 washes. Dry in shade.
♨️
Chanderi & Georgette
Gentle machine wash on delicate cycle or dry clean. Iron on low heat from reverse side with a thin cloth.
