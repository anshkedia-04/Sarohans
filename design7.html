Craft & Fabric

Shop by Weave & Art

Every fabric tells a story. Explore India's most exquisite textile traditions, from the tie-dye mastery of Bandhani to the regal shimmer of pure silk.

Bandhani
Ancient tie-dye craft from Rajasthan. Each piece is unique, formed by thousands of tiny bindings.
Pure Silk
Lustrous Kanjivaram and Banarasi silk woven with gold zari for timeless regal elegance.
Georgette
Light and flowy, perfect for evening occasions with a graceful, flattering drape.
Gota Patti
Royal Rajasthani embroidery. Golden ribbons applied to create stunning geometric patterns.
Handloom
Sustainably woven on traditional looms, preserving India's irreplaceable textile heritage.
